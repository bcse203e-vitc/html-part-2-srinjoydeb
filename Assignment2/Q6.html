Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 300g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, brown the minced beef over medium heat, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pan, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories per serving: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbs: 60g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 5g
